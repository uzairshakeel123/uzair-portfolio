Brazil offers stunning beaches, rich cultural heritage, and a passion for music and dance. Turkey: A bridge between East and West, Turkey boasts ancient ruins, bustling bazaars, and a rich culinary tradition. Its diverse landscapes range from stunning coastlines to the surreal landscapes of Cappadocia.
